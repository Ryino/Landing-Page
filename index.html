Our Idea

The proposed food e-marketplace digitalizes the GIKI campus, addressing a real-life issue by providing a convenient food ordering platform. This innovative solution aims to enhance the overall campus experience for students.

Motivation to work

GIKI’s missing food platform causes slow, cash- only ordering, failing phone orders, vendor management issues, and reimbursement delays. We surveyed students, vendors, faculty, and riders to design a digital food delivery app addressing these problems.

Usage

Vendors, students, and faculty enjoy streamlined services, faster communication, easier access, and improved convenience, enhancing daily life across GIKI campus.
